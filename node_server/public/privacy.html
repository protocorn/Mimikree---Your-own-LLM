Privacy Policy of Mimikree

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email, name, and securely hashed password
  • Social Media Data: Information from GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit, and Medium, including usernames, profile details, repositories, readmes, LinkedIn profiles, tweets, Reddit posts, and Medium articles
  • Calendar Data: Information from Google Calendar, including events, schedules, appointments, and meeting details
  • Self-Assessment Data: Communication style, personality traits, writing samples, and interests
  • Documents: Content from PDF files that you upload
  • Images: Images you upload, their URLs, and descriptions
  • Ratings: Ratings provided by other users about your content

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following ways:

  • User information and metadata are stored in MongoDB
  • Images are stored in Cloudinary's cloud storage
  • Documents and content are processed through our LLaMA server for AI personalization

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used for:

  • Creating a personalized AI model that can respond in your communication style
  • Enabling others to interact with your AI persona
  • Incorporating your calendar availability and scheduling preferences into your AI model
  • Improving the service and user experience

4. Data Security

We implement several security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Authentication is managed using JSON Web Tokens (JWT)
  • Secure connections with MongoDB and third-party services
  • Rate limiting and error handling to prevent abuse

5. User Control

Users have control over their data:

  • You can choose which social profiles to connect
  • You can choose whether to connect your Google Calendar and what calendar data to share
  • You decide which PDFs and images to upload
  • You control what self-assessment information to provide

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudinary: For image storage and management
  • MongoDB: For database storage
  • Google Calendar API: To fetch calendar data and event information
  • External APIs: To fetch data from GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit, and Medium
